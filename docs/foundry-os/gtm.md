# Go-To-Market (GTM) strategy

Marketing within the FoundryOS is treated as a highly constrained experiment. Unlike traditional approaches that scatter efforts across a multitude of platforms, the GTM module enforces extreme focus to generate usable evidence.

## The Single-Channel Rule
Founders must pick ONE unproved channel and test it until a conclusion is reached.
- **One Channel.**
- **One Message.**
- **One Core Action.**

## Marketing as an Experiment
GTM is the Search for Message-Market fit. The process involves:
- Treating initial ad spend as R&D, not a growth engine.
- Launching → Measuring the Cost of Customer Acquisition (CAC) → Deciding.

## SEO Strategy
The OS views SEO as a product strategy, not content generation for a blog.
- **Process:** Identify pain-driven search intent.
- **Execution:** Build specific landing pages answering those queries.
- **Goal:** Establish an organic acquisition moat centered around bottom-of-funnel conversion keywords.

## Deliverables
- Activation-first marketing plan.
- Single channel deployment tracking.
- Conversion tracking scripts (UTM tagging, initial referral monitoring).

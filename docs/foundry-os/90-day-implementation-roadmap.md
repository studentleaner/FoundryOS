# The 90-Day Implementation Roadmap

The **FoundryOS** maps the theoretical 12-Stage Lifecycle into a deterministic, aggressive 90-day sprint. Startups operate under severe constraints of time and capital. This roadmap forces velocity by associating precise week-by-week outputs that map directly to the canonical lifecycle stages.

---

## 📅 Month 1: Validation & Architecture

### **Week 0: Idea Intake & Structural Evaluation**
*Aligns with Stage 1 (Ideation & Intent)*
- **Objective:** Prevent the building of "interesting nonsense." Run the raw idea through the **VentureOS** for structural strength and threat evaluation.
- **Output:** A greenlight to proceed based on a strong differentiation score and high infrastructure depth potential.

### **Week 1-2: Audience Validation & Lean Experiments**
*Aligns with Stage 2 (Validation)*
- **Objective:** Validate behavior over opinions. Do not write backend code yet.
- **Output:** Measurable paid-intent signals, concierge test success, or high-conversion landing page metrics. 

### **Week 3: Business Design & Brand Frameworks**
*Aligns with Stage 3 (Business Design)*
- **Objective:** Define the business model, unit economics, and brand story.
- **Output:** Completed Business Model Canvas, defined LTV:CAC targets, and the brand identity/story ready for implementation.

### **Week 4: MVP Spec & Data Engineering**
*Aligns with Stage 4 (MVP Architecture)*
- **Objective:** Define the minimum features required to drive the core user job-to-be-done. Bootstrap the data infrastructure.
- **Output:** Database schema, API architecture, and a ruthless One-Core-Outcome feature list.

---

## 📅 Month 2: Build, Ship, & Acquire

### **Week 5-6: The Builder Muscle (MVP Build & Ship)**
*Aligns with Stage 4 (MVP Architecture)*
- **Objective:** Rapidly execute the MVP spec. Lock out scope creep.
- **Output:** A functional, end-to-end MVP that allows a user to complete the core outcome within defined SLAs.

### **Week 7: Product Analytics Instrumentation**
*Aligns with Stage 5 (Metrics & Analytics)*
- **Objective:** You cannot improve what you cannot measure. Instrumentation must happen before scaling acquisition.
- **Output:** AARRR funnel tracking implemented, North Star Metric defined, and analytics dashboards live.

### **Week 8: The Traction Engine & Core GTM**
*Aligns with Stage 6 (GTM/SEO)*
- **Objective:** Launch the primary acquisition channel and capture initial users.
- **Output:** Initial user acquisition sequence running, paid/organic funnel activated, and initial CAC measured.

---

## 📅 Month 3: Retain, Scale, & Capitalize

### **Week 9: Retention & Community Cultivation**
*Aligns with Stage 7 (Retention & PMF)*
- **Objective:** Analyze if users are staying. Fix the leaky bucket before pouring more water.
- **Output:** Week 1/Week 4 retention analysis, "Aha" moment optimizations, and initial feedback loops established.

### **Week 10: Business Operations & Revenue Systems**
*Aligns with Stage 8 (Operations & Revenue)*
- **Objective:** Transition from scrappy hustle to repeatable operational rhythm.
- **Output:** RACI matrices, SLA operations defined, billing engines optimized, and revenue tracking locked.

### **Week 11: Scale & Governance**
*Aligns with Stage 9 (Scale) & Stage 10 (Governance)*
- **Objective:** Ensure the infrastructure, team, and corporate structure can handle growth.
- **Output:** Cloud infrastructure load tested, IPs assigned, compliance baseline (GDPR/SOC2) reviewed, and cleanly structured cap tables.

### **Week 12: Investor Readiness & Capital Modeling**
*Aligns with Stage 11 (Fundraising) & Stage 12 (Exit Paths)*
- **Objective:** Translate operational momentum into a compelling, data-backed institutional narrative.
- **Output:** Metric-heavy memos, validated unit economics, 3-scenario financial models, and defined potential liquidity/exit paths.

---

> [!TIP]
> **The Explicit Kill/Pivot Mechanism:** If an explicit output fails to materialize at any week (e.g., zero paid-intent signals by Week 2), the FoundryOS mandates an immediate pivot or kill decision. Do not move to the next week's objectives if the previous week's validation fails.

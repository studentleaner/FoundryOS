# Market Intelligence Crawler

To fuel the Venture Thesis Generator with high-quality data, the SIE relies on a structured **Market Intelligence Crawler**. This OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) framework actively scrapes, normalizes, and categorizes existing global market landscapes.

## The Scraping Ecosystem
The crawler systematically extracts intelligence from authoritative sources to maintain a real-time reality check on competitor density and feature gaps.
- **Product Marketplaces:** Product Hunt, Capterra, G2, AWS Marketplace.
- **Financial & Startup Data:** Crunchbase, SEC Filings, CB Insights.
- **Developer/Research Networks:** GitHub repositories, academic journals (arXiv).
- **Macro/News Analysis:** TechCrunch, Business Insider, Regulatory body reports.

## The Intelligence Extraction Schema
The engine parses raw unstructured data into a rigid taxonomy to analyze every vendor on the market.
- **Domain & Subdomain:** (e.g., Finance -> Accounts Receivable)
- **Capitalization:** Funding stage, Total funding, Revenue models.
- **Technical Coverage:** Integration depth (ATS, CRM, ERP connectors), deployment frameworks (Cloud vs Edge), and core features.
- **Gap Diagnostics:** Explicit logging of "Missing Workflow Stages" and "Compliance Vulnerabilities".

## Anomaly Detection & Triggers
The Intelligence Crawler does not run passively; it is designed to trigger automated alerts when market dynamics aggressively shift:
- **Funding Anomalies:** Rapid capital influxes into obscure subdomains.
- **Regulatory Shifts:** New statutory laws requiring sudden compliance automation (e.g., GDPR, EU AI Act).
- **Adoption Spikes:** Sudden surges in Github stargazers or G2 review counts for specific integration frameworks.

*This intelligence ensures that the Execution OS never commits to building a product in a market that is already mathematically oversaturated or structurally obsolete.*

# Personal Strategic Mode (PSM)

The Strategic Intelligence Engine (SIE) is not a simple "pass/fail" compliance tool. Its primary function is to act as a **Strategic Thinking Amplifier**. This is explicitly handled by the internal **Personal Strategic Mode**.

---

## 1. The Core Purpose of PSM

When an idea is first ingested, it is rarely ready for institutional capital evaluation. If the engine immediately subjected a raw idea to Monte Carlo capital simulation, almost every idea would be rejected. 

Instead, the PSM treats the idea as a formative concept. It uses the intelligence gathered from the Structural Evaluation, Competitor Threat Engine, and Multi-Agent Debate to **expose vulnerabilities and suggest repositioning**.

## 2. PSM Outputs

While in Personal Strategic Mode, the engine does not output an "Expected IRR" or "Invest Probability". It outputs an advisory action plan:

- **Strength Map:** What is fundamentally unique about this idea that incumbents cannot easily replicate?
- **Weakness & Fragility Map:** Where will this idea bleed cash? Where is it over-reliant on third-party platforms?
- **Repositioning Generator:** If the market is too crowded, how can the target audience be shifted to a niche with higher willingness-to-pay and lower competition?
- **Moat Upgrade Framework:** If the idea lacks infrastructure depth, what specific data assets or workflow integrations must be added to build a structural moat?
- **Execution Simplifier:** Identifying complex technical features that do not drive the core outcome, explicitly recommending their removal from the MVP.

---

## 3. The Advisory Tone

The PSM is designed to be **Founder-Friendly but Structurally Honest**. It behaves as a private strategic war room.

> *"Your differentiation relies entirely on a generative UI. If Microsoft bundles this feature, your margin goes to zero. You must pivot to capture proprietary workflow data before launching GTM."*

The goal of PSM is to help the absolute best iteration of the idea emerge before any capital is risked.

# System Roadmap & Extensions

The architecture of the **Strategic Intelligence Engine (SIE)** is designed to be highly modular. By explicitly separating the UI, Intelligence, Simulation, and Data layers, the system can be expanded to support institutional-grade logic without rewriting the core components.

---

## 📅 Roadmap: Advanced Modules

As the database grows and the core structural reasoning improves, the following modules are slated for integration:

### 1. Bayesian Probability Updating
Currently, the Probability Assignment model relies on heuristic rules generated by the Debate Engine. In the future, as real-world outcomes are explicitly tracked in the system, the probabilities will self-correct. 
*Example: If 8 out of 10 startups subjected to an "Aggressive Price War" by an incumbent die within 12 months, the engine will automatically adjust the CRI (Competitor Reaction Intensity) baseline mortality rate.*

### 2. Live API Event Ingestion
Instead of manually updating a competitor Threat Score, the SIE will ingest live signals:
- Funding round announcements (increasing the competitor's *Capital War Chest Score*).
- Repositories moving to Open-Source (increasing the *Clone Speed Score* risk).
- Regulatory rulings (altering the *Regulatory Shock* probability distribution).

### 3. Pricing War Simulator
An isolated economic engine that mathematically models what happens if an incumbent drops prices by 40%. It recalculates the startup's LTV:CAC logic to find the exact month the startup will burn through its remaining cash reserves.

### 4. M&A & Acquisition Probability Engine
A reverse-engineered model that scans the incumbent landscape and calculates the mathematical probability of a Strategic Acquisition based on missing workflow gaps in the incumbent's portfolio.

### 5. Multi-Fund Correlation Engine
Scaling the VC Capital Mode (VCM) from single-portfolio logic to multi-fund strategy logic, allowing sovereign wealth funds or massive LPs to cross-check correlation risks across entirely different asset classes.

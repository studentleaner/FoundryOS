# Continuous Data Room Generator

The traditional startup Data Room is a static Google Drive folder assembled hastily the night before a VC meeting. It is inherently prone to missing data, outdated financials, and unverified codebases. 

The SIE completely automates the due diligence process by generating a real-time, tamper-proof Data Room continuous updated by the 24-Hour Startup OS.

## Core Aggregations

### 1. The Codebase & Architecture Export
- The system automatically links the live GitHub/GitLab repositories.
- Extracts and embeds the high-level system diagrams and workflow engine maps. 
- *Why it matters:* Proves the startup is built on scalable, rational tech, not duct-tape.

### 2. The Telemetry Proof
- Embeds a read-only view of the Analytics Dashboard (Mixpanel/Amplitude).
- Extracts the active Error Rates from Sentry/Datadog.
- *Why it matters:* Investors don't have to trust the founder's slide deck; they can see the live AARRR metrics themselves.

### 3. The Legal & Operations Vault
- Connects to Stripe Atlas / Clerky equivalents to assemble incorporation docs.
- Highlights unassigned IP or missing contractor NDAs.
- Produces a real-time snapshot of the company Cap Table and Employee Stock Option Pool (ESOP).

### 4. The Decision History Log
- A comprehensive log of every Pivot/Continue signal historically triggered by the OS.
- *Why it matters:* Shows investors the team's iteration velocity and resilience based on data.

## The Deliverable
When an investor requests access, the system generates a secure, tokenized URL. The portal presents a unified dashboard containing the Pitch Deck (auto-generated from the Valuation Engine), the Risk Score (from the Risk Engine), and the complete Operational Data Room.
